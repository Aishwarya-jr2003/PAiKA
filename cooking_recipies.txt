Spaghetti Carbonara:
Ingredients: Spaghetti, eggs, parmesan cheese, bacon, black pepper
Instructions: Cook pasta. Fry bacon. Mix eggs and cheese. Combine all with 
pasta while hot. The heat cooks the eggs. Add pepper to taste.
Time: 20 minutes

Chicken Tikka Masala:
Ingredients: Chicken, yogurt, tomatoes, cream, garam masala, ginger, garlic
Instructions: Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices. Grill chicken. Make sauce 
with tomatoes, cream, and spices. Combine and simmer.
Time: 45 minutes
