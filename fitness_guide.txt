Fitness and Exercise Guide

Cardio Exercises:
Running, cycling, swimming are great for cardiovascular health. Aim for 
150 minutes of moderate cardio per week or 75 minutes of vigorous cardio.

Strength Training:
Build muscle with exercises like squats, deadlifts, bench press, and rows.
Train each muscle group 2-3 times per week. Progressive overload is key.

Flexibility:
Stretching and yoga improve flexibility and reduce injury risk. Stretch 
after workouts when muscles are warm.

Nutrition:
Protein: 0.8-1g per pound of bodyweight for muscle growth
Carbs: Primary energy source, especially for intense workouts
Fats: Essential for hormone production, aim for healthy fats

Recovery:
Sleep 7-9 hours per night. Rest days are crucial for muscle recovery.
Hydrate with at least 8 glasses of water daily.

Beginner Routine:
Week 1-4: 3 days per week, full body workouts
Week 5-8: 4 days per week, upper/lower split
Week 9+: Customize based on goals
